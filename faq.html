Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is DreamCash?

DreamCash is a digital rewards platform where users can earn rewards by completing offers, referrals, and approved activities.

2. How do I earn rewards?

You can earn rewards by completing available offers, participating in referral activities, and using eligible earning options provided on the platform.

3. When will my reward be credited?

Rewards are credited only after the offer provider confirms successful completion. Some rewards may be instant, while others may take time for verification.

4. Why was my offer not credited?

An offer may not be credited if it was not completed properly, was rejected by the offer provider, or violated advertiser rules.

5. Can I create multiple accounts?

No. Only one account is allowed per user. Multiple accounts may result in suspension or permanent account termination.

6. Is VPN or Proxy allowed?

No. VPN, proxy, emulator, bots, scripts, or any fraudulent tools are strictly prohibited.

7. How do withdrawals work?

Withdrawals are processed from your available balance and may be subject to verification. Processing time may vary depending on the payment method and review status.
